### Convolutional Photonic Reservoir

This is the repository for the Winning Project of the PushQuantum Hackathon 2025 (Quandela's Challenge). This challenge explored the application of Quantum Machine Learning (QML) in the pricing of complex financial derivatives. The focus was on swaptions, which are options granting the holder the right to enter into an interest rate swap at a future date to manage interest rate volatility.

Participants had to work with a dataset of swaption prices simulated via a machine learning model calibrated on real market data. The objective was to design quantum circuits capable of predicting missing price information, testing the effectiveness of quantum architectures in modeling non-linear financial instruments.
